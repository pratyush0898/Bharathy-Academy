What is Bharathy Academy

Bharathy Academy has been a trusted name in education for over 30 years. We specialize in NIOS (National Institute of Open Schooling), helping students learn at their own pace and convenience. Along with NIOS, we also offer classes for CBSE and State Board students, providing education that suits various needs.
